# 🏛️ Yemen Educational Platform (Yemen-Edu)

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **Yemen-Edu**, the comprehensive digital portal for higher education in Yemen.

## 🌟 Our Mission
To bridge the gap between students and educational institutions by providing a unified, transparent, and accessible digital infrastructure for university coordination, academic directories, and student services.

## 🚀 Key Services
- **Unified Coordination Portal:** A centralized system for university admissions.
- **Academic Directory:** Mapping 50+ universities and 500+ academic programs across Yemen.
- **Verification & Authentication:** Digital services for degree equivalency and document authentication.
- **Interactive Educational Map:** GIS-based visualization of educational institutions in all governorates.

## 📊 Impact in Numbers
- **100k+** Students served.
- **50+** Accredited universities and colleges.
- **500+** Academic programs.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
We leverage modern technologies to build a robust educational ecosystem:
- **Architecture:** Enterprise-grade Cloudflare-based infrastructure.
- **Data:** Open Educational Data APIs.
- **Frontend:** Responsive RTL/Arabic-first interfaces.
